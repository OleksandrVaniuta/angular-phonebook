How to use phonebook

  • Start using

    - You need to create your account or to sign in, if you are already registered, just follow one of the links

  • First visit

    - When you sign in or sign up, you will be taken to the contact page - If this is your first time using the contact book, your contact list will be empty

  • Create your first contact

    - To create your first contact, just click the button with "+" icon in the bottom-right corner of your screen - When you clicked, the modal window will open and you will be able to add contact information (name and number), then click "add" to create contact - Attention! All fields are required

  • Operation with contacts

    - Your created contact is displayed as a card - In the top-right corner of card you can click on "three-dots" button - Finally, the drop-down list will open and you will see possible operations with contacts
